Take a look at our services

Emergency Services

Provides immediate treatment for acute illnesses, injuries, and trauma that require urgent care.

Cardiology Services

Specializes in diagnosing and treating heart-related conditions, including heart attacks, arrhythmias, and heart failure.

Surgical Services

Includes operating rooms where surgical procedures are performed, ranging from minor surgeries to complex operations.

Pharmacy Services

Dispenses medications prescribed by doctors and provides essential advice on their proper use, dosage, and potential side effects.

Neurosurgery and Neurology

Specializes in the treatment of brain, spine, and nervous system disorders, including surgeries and non-surgical treatments.

Radiology and Imaging

Provides diagnostic imaging services such as X-rays, MRIs, CT scans, and ultrasounds to help diagnose medical conditions.

Laboratory Services

Conducts various tests on blood, tissue, and other bodily fluids to diagnose and monitor health conditions.

Inpatient Services

Offers care for patients who need to stay in the hospital overnight or longer, including medical, surgical, and intensive care units.

Outpatient Services

Provides care for patients who do not require an overnight stay, including consultations, minor procedures, and follow-up visits.

Dialysis Services

Focuses on childbirth and the care of newborns, including prenatal, delivery, and postnatal care.Provides treatment for patients with kidney failure through hemodialysis or peritoneal dialysis.

Mental Health Services

Provides psychiatric care, counseling, and therapy for patients with mental health conditions, develop coping strategies, helping them manage symptoms and improve emotional well-being.

Medical Records and Health Information

Manages patients' medical records and ensures the confidentiality, accuracy, and secure storage of health information. This inludes maintaining compliance with healthcare regulations.
